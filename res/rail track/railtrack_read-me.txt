--------------------railtrack; Read Me-------------------------

//Contents.
low poly rail track model. for games and realtime applications
Models are in their native .MAX format. Textures are in .TGA format, and include diffuse, specular and normal maps.
Models are free for non-commercial use only, and are licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution-Non-commercial-No Derivative Works 3.0 License.

//Details.

this was really quickly done, as a favour for someone. so the quality is not fantastic. you may want to touch up some of the textures or something.
